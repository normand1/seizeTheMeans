Seize The Means of Education!

In a disgusting effort to flaunt their wealth and priviledge the bourgeoisie have made education free and widely available. The grand Communist tradition of waiting in lines for free products has recently been undermined my a movement of capitalist sympathizer University professors posting their lectures for free online. There are now many capitalist learning institutions that offer free courses. These courses are taught in multiple languages and focus on a variety of subjects from bourgeois pursuits like Science, Technology and Math to respectable socialist subjects like modern art appreciation. Below, I've compiled a list of web sites where you can seize the means of education from the Capitalist pigs and take a free online course!

Website Cost Categories
edx.org Free (offers paid certificates) Computer Science, Language, Data Science, Business and Management, Engineering, Humanities
coursera.org Free (offers paid certificates) Computer Science, Language, Data Science, Business and Management, Engineering, Humanities, Health, Math and Logic
futurelearn.com Free (offers paid certificates) Computer Science, Language, Business and Management, Engineering, Health, Math and Logic, Law, History, Creative Arts and Media, Nature and Environment

Now at this point you might be asking: "Lemmy, I'm a socialist and I reject all modern conveniences like computers. I think the state should own all proprty so I refuse to purchase a computer (even this $72 computer!)"

Comrade, I would say to you I understand your plight! I myself reject the ownership of private property and that is why I am typing this at a shared library computer. With a passion for revolution, I waited joyfully for 3 hours for a downtrodden peasant to finish browsing pornography and reading the Huffington Post on this shared terminal and now I am taking my turn the way Stalin intended. You also can find a local library in which you can wait in line and meditate on Mao's sacrifices here. If you lack the revolutionary fervor that I posses then there are a number of capitalist non-profits that you can seize a computer from (by request).
